Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment? expand_more

You can book appointments directly from a doctor's profile page. Look for the 'Book Appointment' button and follow the on-screen instructions to select a date and time.

How can I edit my information? expand_more

To edit your personal information, navigate to the 'My Profile' section from the main menu. Here you can update your name, contact details, and other personal data.

Is my data secure? expand_more

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. All personal data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with industry standards. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details.
